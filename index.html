What about the map?

Forza Horizon 5 takes us on a journey to Mexico - the most extensive map in the history of the series, about 50% larger than the one included in Forza Horizon 4. It features a desert, rainforest, ancient ruins, a huge canyon, picturesque beaches, as well as villages and the city of Guanajuato. Additionally, the highest point in the history of the series - the top of a volcano - awaits to be conquered here.
